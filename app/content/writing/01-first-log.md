## Return to Text

Modern interfaces have abandoned the text in favor of the icon. We are swimming in a sea of ambiguous glyphs, frosted glass, and meaningless drop shadows. 

We believe that text is the ultimate interface. The terminal represents absolute control and absolute clarity. 

### Why ASCII?

Because it forces constraint. When you are limited to the characters on your keyboard, you cannot hide bad structure behind pretty gradients. The architecture must stand on its own.

```python
def philosophy():
    return "Build what is necessary. Discard what is not."
```

We are building taohq as a reflection of this ethos. It is raw, it is fast, and it does not apologize for its sharp edges.
